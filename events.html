Founded - Start of 2018

February 2018

N.O.W. @nowmozillaclub 31m
Not the movie, but rather the start of something amalgamated from an idea. Add three more like-minded individuals in the mix, and here we are! N.O.W. was started in our junior year by the four of us, now in our senior years. Oh, how time flies.

March 2018

N.O.W. @nowmozillaclub 31m
Our first event: teach attendees to make the classical game of Snake using JavaScript. Students were taught the basics of what web development pertains to, and how they can contribute towards it with the tools available at their disposal.

March 2018

N.O.W. @nowmozillaclub 31m
One event down, we agreed it was time to bring more people into the fold; we kept the recruitments for people where they join one of four cells suited to their skills: Technical, Logistics, Advertising, and Creative.

April 2018

N.O.W. @nowmozillaclub 31m
This was a big one! Working closely with the Computer Engineering Department of the college, we organized the Final Year Project Competition for the senior year students of the aforementioned department. All suit and a crisp tie makes Jack look quite professional.

April 2018

N.O.W. @nowmozillaclub 31m
The second event to be working in collaboration with the Computer Engineering Department, we organized this event where the sophomore students could submit their Android applications for the titular competition. This followed Saavishkaar 2018, and made up quite the hectic three days!

September 2018

N.O.W. @nowmozillaclub 31m
A new year rolled in, and with it, we decided to expand ourselves. With our aim to target the freshmen and sophomore students, we kept this time’s recruitments over a two-day period, just like last time.

October 2018

N.O.W. @nowmozillaclub 31m
Our first event in our senior year, this workshop focused on teaching students the basics of operating GitHub, and how version control could factor in the lifecycle of software development. We also introduced attendees to Git, and had a fun little competition with a little prize at the end!
